11 Plastic ALTERNATIVE PRODUCTS FOR YOU

Cotton bag

No more Plastic bags!Cotton bags only take less than 4 months to degrade in a landfill while plastic bags take 500 years. Cotton bags can be reused thousands of times and are machine washable. While cotton bags are more durable, they also require less energy to make when compared to plastic bags.

Wooden Cutlery

Portable cutlery set, can be made of metal or wood. They are small and compact, you can carry them to anywhere, just like carrying your own water bottle.

Reusable Bottle

Plastic bottles are the major plastic waste. Instead, bring your own bottle are more eco-friendly.

Glass Container

Storing food in glass containers is clean and also better for your health when you reheat the food. If your refrigerator doesn’t have enough space to fit all the glass containers, beeswax food wraps are a good choice because they are reusable and washable.

Metal Reusable Capsule

Refillable coffee pods don’t have to be discarded after use, so if you use a coffee machine to make your morning drink everyday, you save at least 7 plastic coffee capsules by using the refillable one.

Bamboo Cotton Bud

Bamboo cotton buds are biodegradable and home compostable. Bamboo cotton buds also require less resources to make compared to the plastic one.

Bamboo Toothbrush & Homemade Toothpaste

Bamboo toothbrushes have a biodegradable handle that easily deteriorates. The antibacterial properties of bamboo allow it to cultivate without using toxics. Some bamboo toothbrushes allow you to change to a new toothbrush head. So you can save from 3 - 10 plastic toothbrushes every year.

Wooden Comb

Plastic combs and brushes create static electricity on your hair, wooden combs don't conduct electricity, so they protect your hair from static electricity.

Soap

While liquid shampoo and washing gel are packed in large plastic bottles, soap using only paper for packaging and leave no plastic behind.

Wooden Clipper

Plastic laundry clippers have a short life and are easily damaged. Plastics and poor quality metals are bad for the environment and, unlike wood, they are not renewable.

Refillable Lighter

The refillable lighter is more textural than a plastic lighter, and will be of better quality and safer to use.
